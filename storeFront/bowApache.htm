Apache Bow and Arrow

This Apache-made bow and arrow was created from desert wood and painted with red and yellow power signs. The arrow has a Picture Jasper arrow head and the shaft is painted with a lightning bolt to make it swift. This wonderful piece has a leather wrapped grip on the bow and is finished with beads, turquoise and braided sinew.
