Creek Warclub with Dreamcatcher

This warclub was made from a buffalo jawbone, handmade; the glass beading and finish are unique to the Creek. The head is finished with a dreamcatcher, brass tacks on both sides, leather, horsehair, feathers and glass beads. Leather color, fur, beads, and feathers will vary according to tribe.

Apache Bow and Arrow

This Apache-made bow and arrow was created from desert wood and painted with red and yellow power signs. The arrow has a Picture Jasper arrow head and the shaft is painted with a lightning bolt to make it swift. This wonderful piece has a leather wrapped grip on the bow and is finished with beads, turquoise and braided sinew.

Cherokee Deer Foot Knives

Our most popular knife is this deer foot knife with bone tip. The knife is finished with a rawhide wrap at hilt. Hilt is accented with brass studs. Handmade by a Cherokee Indian from my tribe.
